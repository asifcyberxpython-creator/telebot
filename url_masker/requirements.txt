Here are 20 very simple, awareness-focused quiz questions on social media safety & cybercrime, suitable for students and clearly useful for their personal life + future careers.

You can ask orally, show on slides, or print as a handout.


---

Cybercrime Awareness Quiz (20 Questions)

1.

Keeping your social media account public mainly increases the risk of:
A) More followers
B) Identity theft
C) Better reach
D) Faster internet

✅ Answer: B


---

2.

Which information should NOT be shared publicly?
A) Hobbies
B) Phone number
C) Favorite movie
D) Food habits

✅ Answer: B


---

3.

Posting your live location can lead to:
A) Cyber bullying
B) Physical stalking
C) Account verification
D) Better engagement

✅ Answer: B


---

4.

Accepting friend requests from strangers is risky because:
A) It increases followers
B) They may misuse your data
C) It slows your phone
D) It improves networking

✅ Answer: B


---

5.

Which profile photo is unsafe?
A) Nature photo
B) Cartoon avatar
C) Photo with office/school ID
D) Logo

✅ Answer: C


---

6.

Using the same password for all social media accounts is:
A) Safe
B) Recommended
C) Risky
D) Mandatory

✅ Answer: C


---

7.

Two-factor authentication (2FA) helps to:
A) Increase followers
B) Improve privacy
C) Protect accounts
D) Upload faster

✅ Answer: C


---

8.

Which message is most likely a scam?
A) “Hi, how are you?”
B) “Good morning”
C) “You won a prize, click here”
D) “Let’s meet tomorrow”

✅ Answer: C


---

9.

Sharing OTP with anyone is:
A) Normal
B) Sometimes okay
C) Very dangerous
D) Required

✅ Answer: C


---

10.

Social media quizzes mainly collect:
A) Entertainment value
B) Your personal data
C) AI skills
D) Nothing important

✅ Answer: B


---

11.

Posting angry or offensive content online can affect:
A) Only friends
B) Only followers
C) Job opportunities
D) Nothing

✅ Answer: C


---

12.

AI can misuse your public photos for:
A) Filters only
B) Deepfake creation
C) Storage
D) Backup

✅ Answer: B


---

13.

Which is the safest action after receiving a suspicious link?
A) Click it
B) Forward it
C) Ignore and report
D) Reply angrily

✅ Answer: C


---

14.

Deleting a post means it is:
A) Gone forever
B) Still possibly retrievable
C) Legally safe
D) Invisible to police

✅ Answer: B


---

15.

Cyber criminals mostly target people who:
A) Are rich
B) Are famous
C) Overshare online
D) Are students only

✅ Answer: C


---

16.

Sharing screenshots of private chats without consent is:
A) Legal
B) Ethical
C) Illegal
D) Encouraged

✅ Answer: C


---

17.

Which habit improves cyber safety the MOST?
A) Posting daily
B) Strong passwords
C) Public profiles
D) Accepting all requests

✅ Answer: B


---

18.

Fake profiles are commonly used for:
A) Friendship
B) Entertainment
C) Fraud and impersonation
D) Learning

✅ Answer: C


---

19.

Background verification for jobs may include checking:
A) Exam marks
B) Social media activity
C) Phone wallpaper
D) Chat history

✅ Answer: B


---

20.

Cybercrime usually starts with:
A) Advanced hacking
B) AI programming
C) Small online mistakes
D) Government systems

✅ Answer: C
